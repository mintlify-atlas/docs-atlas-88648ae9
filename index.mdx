Ocipe turns the chaotic experience of managing recipes across apps, bookmarks, and screenshots into a single organized place. Add recipes manually or auto-fill them from any recipe URL using Google Gemini, then let the grocery planner do the heavy lifting when shopping time comes around.

## What's inside Ocipe

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Cookbook" icon="utensils">
    Store all your personal recipes with ingredients, cooking notes, and frequency labels. Search by name, category, or ingredients you already have.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fridge" icon="snowflake">
    Track what ingredients you currently have at home. Ocipe uses your fridge inventory to subtract items from generated grocery lists automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Grocery Planner" icon="cart-shopping">
    Select recipes for the week, optionally randomize when you're indecisive, and get an instant aggregated shopping list — minus what's already in your fridge.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Get started in 4 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Register at [ocipe.vercel.app](https://ocipe.vercel.app) or run the app locally by following the [Quickstart](/quickstart) guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your recipes">
    Head to the Recipes page and add recipes manually, or paste a URL to have Google Gemini auto-fill the form for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stock your fridge">
    Open the [Fridge](/features/fridge) page and add the ingredients you currently have at home, organized into groups.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan your grocery run">
    Go to [Grocery Planner](/features/grocery-planner), select the recipes you want to cook this week, and get a ready-to-use shopping list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
